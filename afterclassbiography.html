🌟 Introduction

.

Babar Azam is one of the finest cricketers Pakistan has ever produced. Known for his elegant cover drives, consistency, and calm personality, he has become a role model for millions of cricket fans around the world. 🌍✨

.

👶 Early Life & Cricket Journey

.
Babar Azam was born in Lahore and developed a love for cricket at a very young age. ❤️🏏 Inspired by his cousins, the Akmal brothers, he joined a cricket academy and spent countless hours practicing. His hard work and dedication paid off and, He represented Pakistan at the Under-15 and Under-19 levels before making his international debut. 🚀.
🇵🇰 International Career

.

ODI Debut: 2015 vs Zimbabwe 📅.

Debut Match: Pakistan vs Zimbabwe.
📍 Venue: Gaddafi Stadium, Lahore.
🎯 Score on Debut: 54 runs.


⭐ He scored a half-century in his very first ODI innings, making an impressive start to his international career. since his debut, Babar has become one of the most reliable batsmen in world cricket. He has captained Pakistan in all three formats and has broken numerous records. 🏆.
