Weekly Healthy Meal Prep Guide

This page shows a simple weekly nutrition routine. Each card has its own content area, padding, border, and margin.

Breakfast Bowls

Prepare overnight oats with chia seeds, mixed berries, oat milk, and a drizzle of honey.

Time: 10 minutes

Green Smoothies

Blend fresh spinach, frozen bananas, protein powder, and almond milk for quick energy.

Time: 05 minutes

Protein Prep

Bake chicken breasts, tofu blocks, or lean turkey mince with garlic and olive oil.

Time: 30 minutes

Grain Batches

Cook a large pot of quinoa or brown rice to use as bases throughout the week.

Time: 20 minutes

Roasted Veggies

Chop and roast sweet potatoes, broccoli, and bell peppers with herbs and spices.

Time: 25 minutes

Smart Snacking

Portion out raw almonds, walnut halves, pumpkin seeds, and dark chocolate squares.

Time: 10 minutes

Fitness Tip: Good nutrition requires smart planning, whole foods, healthy fats, and consistency.
