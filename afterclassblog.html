Building a Healthy Plate

Why a Balanced Meal Matters

🍽️ A healthy plate is built by filling half of your plate with fruits and vegetables 🥗🥕🍎, one quarter with protein such as chicken, eggs, fish, or beans 🍗🥚🐟🫘, and the other quarter with whole grains like rice or bread 🌾🍚🍞. Add small amounts of healthy fats 🥑🥜 and drink plenty of water 💧 to keep your body healthy and full of energy ⚡.

Learn more about healthy eating here: Guide for Nutrition

Smart Snack Choices

🍎🥜 Choosing healthy snacks can help us stay energized and support good health. Foods such as fresh fruits, nuts, sprouts, and yogurt provide important nutrients and are better choices than chips and sugary drinks 🥗🥤.

Explore more nutrition facts here: Nutrition Tips

Nutrition Tips for Students

Drink Enough Water

💧 Water helps the body stay healthy, improves digestion, and helps the brain stay focused. Students should carry a water bottle and drink enough water throughout the day.

Eat Colourful Food

🌈 Different colors in fruits and vegetables provide different nutrients. A colorful plate often means a healthier and more balanced plate 🥗🍎🥕.
